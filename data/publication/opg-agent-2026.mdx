We propose OPGAgent, a multi-tool agentic system for auditable dental panoramic X-ray (OPG) interpretation. OPGAgent coordinates specialized perception modules with a consensus mechanism through three components: Hierarchical Evidence Gathering, a Specialized Toolbox, and a Consensus Subagent. On OPG-Bench and the public MMOral-OPG benchmark, OPGAgent outperforms current dental VLMs and medical agent frameworks.
